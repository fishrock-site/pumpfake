About Schedule Venue

The Christmas Classic is a basketball tournament for members of the Charlottesville community. The inaugural event will be held Saturday, December 20th, 2025 at the UVA Aquatic and Fitness Center (680 Alderman Rd, Charlottesville, VA 22903). The questions below outline specifics for players and attendees.

What is the format?
We expect 8 teams of 4 or more players. Each game will be full court and 4-on-4. Teams will play two preliminary games for seeding in a single-elimination afternoon playoff culminating in a championship game. Teams will play a minimum of three games and a maximum of five games total.
What are the specific rules for the games?
Games follow standard basketball rules, slightly modified for tournament play, including shortened 10 min halves and a running clock. Detailed rules will be given immediately prior to the event.
Are there awards for winning teams and players?
Yes, the winning team will be awarded (trophy and custom tee shirts) as well as a couple of individual player awards.
Will there be referees?
Yes, each game will have referees.
Is there parking available at the venue?
Limited paid parking is available on-site, but ideally teams can carpool or be dropped off.
What time should players arrive?
Players should arrive at 12pm for registration, warm-up, and gear distribution.
What time will the event end?
The championship game will end around 4:00 pm.
What food will be provided?
No food or drink other than water will be provided at this time.
